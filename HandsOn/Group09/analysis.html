Dataset Analysis

Finally, the dataset referring to the minor contracts carried out in Madrid during the year 2020 has been chosen.

The data is composed of:

  • 2654 rows
  • 12 variables
    • Recognition number field
      • 'M2020' + 5 numbers starting from 00001 to 02662
    • File number
      • String of characters and numbers
    • Contracting section
    • Contracting body
      • Whether it is the director, coordinator, manager, etc.
    • Object of the contract
      • Different nature
    • Type of contract
      • Categorical nature: services, works, supplies or others
    • NIF
      • Corresponding to the contractor company
    • Amount
      • Numerical, in euros from 0€ as a collaboration generally, to 48.349,82€
    • Date of approval
      • All of them approved in 2019
    • Term
      • Numerical, ranging from 0 to 12
    • Date of communication of the registration
      • From 2 of January 2020 to the 30 of June of this same year

License Analysis

License of the original dataset

  • The dataset is published by the Madrid City Council in its own Open Data Portal
  • The license establish that its reuse is allowed for both commercial and non-commercial purposes, understanding reuse as the use of Madrid City Council's own documents for any activity that does not come from a public administration

The license of our future application must include at least the points covered by the original license. A license that satisfies these conditions and has extensive use could be the Creative Commons Atributtion 4.0. It allows to:

  • Share — copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format
  • Adapt — remix, transform, and build upon the material or any purpose, even commercially

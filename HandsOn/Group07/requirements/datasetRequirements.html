Dataset Requirements

  • R1. Data are related to European Union public procurement.

    • cordis-h2020projects.csv. This dataset contains projects and related organisations funded by European Union under the Horizon 2020 framework programme for research and innovation from 2014 to 2020. For more information click on the link.
    • cordis-h2020organizations.csv. The dataset contains information about the participating organisations. For detailed information click on the link.
    • cordis-h2020-msca-fellows.xlsx. This dataset lists the researchers working on projects funded by the Marie Sk≈Çodowska-Curie actions under the Horizon 2020 framework programme (H2020). For more information click on the link.

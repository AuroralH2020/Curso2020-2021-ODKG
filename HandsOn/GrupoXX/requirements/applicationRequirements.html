- Application Covid-19 The application will be able to show the information regarding the budget allocated, in this case, by the Murcian health administration, for the creation of emergency contracts to combat the Covid-19 pandemic. Differentiating between the type of products and the quantity available and pending receipt. - Application translation costs The application would allow searching between the different payments made by the Blaby District Council for the services of transcription and/or translation, showing the date of the payment, department, payee, language and amount. The user would also be allowed to filter by the different types of variables.
